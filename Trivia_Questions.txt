At the 2024 Paris Olympics, a historic live podium photo went viral showing an unprecedented sweep for the floor exercise—marking the first time in Olympic gymnastics history that all three athletes standing on the podium during the ceremony were Black women. Which three athletes were standing on the podium when that iconic photo was taken?
* Answer: Rebeca Andrade (Gold, Brazil), Simone Biles (Silver, USA), and Jordan Chiles (Bronze, USA). (Note: The image of Biles and Chiles bowing down to Andrade on the podium became a global symbol of sisterhood and joy.)
* Gabby Douglas, Sunisa Lee, and Shilese Jones

In 1872, Black community leaders in Texas pooled $800 together to purchase 10 acres of land specifically to host Juneteenth celebrations safely, creating the oldest public park in Texas. What is the name of this historic park located in Houston?
* Answer: Emancipation Park. (Note: During segregation, Black Americans were banned from public parks, so they bought their own land to celebrate the holiday freely).
* Freedom Plaza 
* Jubilee Grounds
